Flores Island, Portugal
Portugal's Flores Island is known locally as the "cape of flowers" because the area is just teeming with unique and colorful flora. The island also features natural hot springs, lagoons, flood rice fields, cozy villages, lakes, volcanoes and world-famous caves.

Zao Fox Village, Japan
The Miyagi Zao mountain isn't just scenic, the area also plays host to the Zao Fox Village. A place where you can hang out with six different species of adorable foxes. Make your trip unforgettable by visiting these wily animals as you take in the breathtaking views.
Ko Adang, Thailand
Still under the radar of most tourists is the beautiful Thai island of Ko Adang. The 30-square kilometer island located within Tarutao National Marine Park has beautiful white sand beaches, stunning coral reefs and lush hills that is just a joy to explore.
